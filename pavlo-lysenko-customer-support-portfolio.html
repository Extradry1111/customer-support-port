    01

    Account Locked/Security is the real problem, not KYC. Highest volume goes to KYC, but customers tolerate that wait. This category has the lowest CSAT and the highest reopen rate of any of the seven.

    02

    Reopens are the single biggest CSAT lever in the dataset, a bigger gap than between any two categories. The highest-leverage fix isn't speed, it's resolving fully on the first reply.

    03

    Channel doesn't explain CSAT, category does. Live Chat carries 57% of volume but scores almost identically to Email (3.81 vs 3.80). The lever to pull is process, not infrastructure.

    01

    Build a dedicated de-escalation playbook for Account Locked/Security: lowest CSAT, highest reopen rate, and usually an already-frustrated customer before it reaches an agent.

    02

    Investigate why Withdrawal Issue and Account Locked/Security reopen most (10.0% and 11.4%): likely vague timeframes, or fixing the symptom without confirming the cause is resolved.

    03

    Don't prioritize speeding up KYC or Wrong Network Deposit. Both are inherently slow, and the data shows customers tolerate the wait when it's communicated clearly.
